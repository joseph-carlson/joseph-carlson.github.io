--- 
1. The Current University System

- Federal government guaranteeing student loans

Summary
- Benefits
    1. Community of young like-minded people
    2. Professional network on departure
    3. Structured learning path through the foundation

- Drawbacks
    1. Outdated learning material
    2. The fluff
    3. Loans in the gaps

- Situation Dependent Cost Benefit

The conclusion reached after a well thought out cost-benefit analysis for the traditional or non-traditional path to higher education is entirely situationally dependent. If you have the opportunity to attend college without accruing a significant amount of student loans, I say go for it.

Looking back, I didn't value the time I had working part time while in college to fully dedicate on my studies. Now that I'm a couple years into the 40-hour work week
